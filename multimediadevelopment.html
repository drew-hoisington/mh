Multimedia Development

Multimedia lends creative tools that can be used to design interactive lessons. This section includes artifacts from my coursework and field experiences that demonstrate the multimedia tools and strategies used to guide other professionals. These artifacts will serve as a window into how these multimedia tools can develop lessons that are engaging for educators and students.
