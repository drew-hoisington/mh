Leadership

Leadership shows initiative, inspiration, and support of a group and their goals. This section includes artifacts from my coursework that demonstrate the theme of leadership, both in my coursework and field experiences. These artifacts will serve as a window into how I demonstrated leadership through technology integration and support for students and other individuals.
