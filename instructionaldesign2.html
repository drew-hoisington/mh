Instructional Design

Instructional Design is the creation of resources and materials for all different types of learning enviornments and experiences. This section includes artifacts from my coursework that demonstrate the instructional design opportunitites from my coursework and field experiences. These artifacts will serve as a window into how learning opportunities are formulated for educators and students.

 

Course Artifacts

MILITARY TRAINING AND RESOURCES COURSE
Course: Instructional Design 5150:631
This was a group project to create a Military Training and Resources course for Stark State College. This group project included Jessica Aubley, Valerie Coughlin, and Katie Lovick. The final product was a Google Site, with videos recorded in Camtasia, a lecture recorded in Articulate Storyline, and the use of Google Forms for assessments.
