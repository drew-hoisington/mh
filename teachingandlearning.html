Teaching and Learning

Support of educators and students is vital to their growth in understanding how technology can enhance education. This section includes artifacts from my coursework and field experiences that demonstrate this support both inside and outside of the classroom. These artifacts will serve as a window into how I demonstrated support for educators, implemented effective uses of technology for students, and worked to enhance the classroom experience.

 

Course Artifacts

INTRODUCTION TO TECHNOLOGY WIKI REFLECTIONS
Course: Intro to Instructional Technology 5150:610
This was a set of reflections we composed during each module of learning in the course. These reflections detail various uses of technology inside and outside of the classroom.
 
INTEGRATING AND IMPLEMENTING TECHNOLOGY PROJECT
Course: Integrating and Implementing Technology 5150:638
For this project, I partnered with Jessica Aubley and using an online platform (Thinglink) that allowed for the integration of images, audio, and video in a presentation. Educators or students can use this platform to interact with a specific image and then embed it into their lecture or on Google Classroom for student interaction and engagement.
 
INTEGRATING AND IMPLEMENTING TECHNOLOGY PAPER AND LESSON PLAN
Course: Integrating and Implementing Technology 5150:638
For this project, I partnered with Jessica Aubley and we created a lesson for my 9th grade World History class at Nordonia High School in Macedonia, Ohio. We used an online platform (Thinglink) during a lesson about World War II. The lesson focused on propaganda Dr. Seuss created during the war and students used the tool to interact and explain his meaning behind each his images.
 
EDUCATIONAL PSYCHOLOGY PAPER
Course: Educational Psychology 5100:624
For this project, I researched the motivations of high school athletes. The focus of this project was to dive into the motivation of my 2019 high school baseball team. This was a group of 19 high school athletes, who were coming off a sub-par season in 2018. I looked at several pyschological theories and how they may have impacted their 2019 baseball season.
 
USING RESEARCH TO INFORM PRACTICE PAPER
Course: Using Research to Inform Practice 5100:640
For this paper, I researched how the advancement of virtual reality could be used to enhance lessons in the classroom. I posed several questions and used the research from the topic to learn if this technology could bring more student engagement to the classroom.
